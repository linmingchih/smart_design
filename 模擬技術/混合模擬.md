混合模擬
---

AEDT平台設計類型可以從設計編輯環境與Solver排列組合而得

Solver包括：
    - HFSS FEM
    - HFSS PI
    - SIwave SYZ
    - SIwave DCIR
    - Q2D
    - Q3D
    - Circuit
    - Icepak
    - IcepakFEA

編輯環境包括：
    - **3D**
    - **Layout**
    - **Schematic**
    - **SIWave**

按照其常用為
    - HFSS FEM-**3D**
    - HFSS FEM-**Layout**
    - HFSS PI-**Layout**
    - SIwave SYZ-**Layout**
    - Circuit-Schematic
    - Q3D-3D
    - Q3D-Layout
    - Q2D-3D
    - Siwave SYZ-SIwave
    - SIwace DCIR-SIwave
    - SIwave SYZ-Layout
    - SIwave DCIR-Layout
    - Icepak-3D

### HFSS Region
    - SIwave SYZ-Layout(HFSS FEM-3D)
    - SIwave SYZ-SIwave(HFSS FEM-3D)


### 子設計 (.aedcomp, .aedbcomp)
    - HFSS FEM-3D(HFSS FEM-3D) 
    - HFSS FEM-Layout(HFSS FEM-Layout)
    - HFSS FEM-3D(HFSS FEM-Layout)
    - HFSS FEM-Layout(HFSS FEM-3D)
    - Q3D-3D(Q3D-3D)
    - Icepak-3D(Icepak-3D)
    - Circuit-Schematic(Circuit-Schematic)

### Dynamic Link (Push Excitation):
    - Circuit[LNA](HFSS FEM-3D)
    - Circuit[LNA](HFSS FEM-Layout)
    - Circuit[Transient](HFSS FEM-3D)
    - Circuit[Transient](HFSS FEM-Layout)
    - Circuit[Transient](Q3D-3D)
    - Circuit[Transient](Q2D-3D)

### Electro Thermal
    - Icepak-3D(SIwave DCIR-Layout)
    - Icepak-3D(HFSS FEM-3D)
